Projects

  1. Relapse-related genes and their interactinos in breast cancer
    Analyzed gene expression datasets to understand biological pathways and decide treatment strategy. We utilized the logistic-accelerated failure time (AFT) mixture regression model (Chen et al., 2013) to analyze the distant metastasis free survival (DMFS) time associated with gene expression levels.

  2. Association between NGS-SNP and overall survival in HR-positive breast cancer patients
    Detected significantly related SNPs in single-locus association analysis with logistic regression test. The results of survival analysis revealed the close association SNPs with survival of breast cancer patients.

  3. Statistical methodology development
    Developed a simulation-based approach and applied to the clinical trial of Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS).

  4. PCOS trial NCT00172523
    Contributed to design clinical trial protocols and analyzed clinical data.

  5. GENIUS study NCT01579630
    Analyzed survival data in a multiple regression of the Cox model. The results were presented in American Society of Clinical Oncology (ASCO) and World Conference on Lung Cancer (WCLC) in 2015.

  6. Circulating tumor cells with colorectal cancer
    Determined an optimal cut-off point in ROC analysis as the best experimental condition for the clinical study.

  7. In-house website construction for calculating sample sizes
    Worked with colleagues on building an in-house website for sample size calculations in a variety of designs.
